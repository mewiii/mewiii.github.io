Saturday, October 14, 2023 - Solar Eclipse

Photographs from Berea College in Berea, KY

The weather in Berea Kentucky this day was very uncooperative. It was mostly cloudy and rainy. We were able to take several pictures when clouds thinned or opened up.

All times are local time.

  • ~1345 - just after eclipse started
  • ~1208 - just after eclipse started
  • ~1327 - several minutes after maximum eclipse

All photographs by Deanna M. Wilborne.
