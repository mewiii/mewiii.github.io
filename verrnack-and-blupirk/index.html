The Adventures of Verrnack & Blupirk

The Adventures of Verrnack & Blupirk is a minimalist science-fiction comic about two extraterrestrial observers attempting to make sense of human logic, customs, and decision-making. Through short, self-contained panels, the comic explores misunderstandings, overconfidence, premature conclusions, and the quiet absurdities that arise when simple rules are applied to complex systems. The series avoids politics, religion, violence, and sexuality, focusing instead on curiosity, humor, and the universal challenge of interpreting a world that rarely behaves as expected.
